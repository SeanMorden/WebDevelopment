Property Details

Whitefield Farm; discover a unique 76 acre Organic hobby farm offering an "Off-Grid" living/working experience in the heart of farming & cottage country. An outstanding open concept Straw Bale home built in 2012 with several outbuildings including a driveshed with carport, unique 40’ diameter round building, bank barn with horse stalls/heated shop. This farm is a prime example of sustainability & self-sufficiency using reclaimed & recycled materials thru-out. Post & beam construction, earthen plaster walls provide maximum heating & cooling, “Truth Window”, quality fiberglass windows/doors + a premium ESSE cookstove. The prep kitchen + serving kitchen overlooks a courtyard herb garden. A luxurious 2nd floor master with 4 pc bath. A covered breezeway with roughed-in heated floors leads to a 4 season sunroom! With the benefits of solar power, propane/generator back up & hot water rads paired with modern amenities of Fibreoptics, ECO-Bees & Verve Lighting System; Live off-grid but on-line!
